Data source: World Air Quality Index (WAQI) project, CPCB Bengaluru stations. Forecasts are produced by a statistical time-series model (Holt-Winters / Holt's linear trend depending on history length) and are estimates, not guarantees. Government monitoring stations occasionally stop reporting for extended periods — when that happens for a station, its "last observed" time will say so and its forecast falls back to a flat estimate rather than inventing trend data. Pipeline runs automatically every hour via GitHub Actions.
